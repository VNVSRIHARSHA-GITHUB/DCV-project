How to Use

Use the controls above to pick a **disease** and **year**. Click **Open Map** to navigate to the national choropleth map. From the map, you can click any state to view its detailed report.

Dataset Scope

  • **Diseases:** Multiple common reportable conditions.
  • **Geography:** All 50 U.S. states plus territories.
  • **Metrics:** Cases, Population, and calculated Rate per 100k.
